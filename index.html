Guide to ZenTune

           

                From deep focus to deep sleep — soundscapes for every mood, every moment.

           
                   
  • Mood: Select a category to match your desired state of mind.
    •                
  • Focus Goal: Set your target session time (minutes).
    •                
  • Calibrate: Generate a curated soundscape to start.
    •                
  • Keep Playing: Enables 'Flow Mode' to auto-play the next track.
    •                
  • Insights: View your session history, total time, and streaks.
